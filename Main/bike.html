WELCOME TO THUNDER MOUNTAIN BIKES, YOUR ONE-STOP BIKE SHOP IN SEDONA, ARIZONA!

We are a locally and independently owned bike shop, centrally located in Sedona, Arizona with views of Thunder Mountain from our front door.   Our building has been a bike shop for over 25 years, and Sedona’s legendary mountain biking history is etched in our walls.   

While a few iterations have come and gone, and we continue to evolve, you can count on Thunder Mountain Bikes to faithfully carry on the Sedona experience.
